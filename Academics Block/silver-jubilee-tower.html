Silver Jubilee Tower

Silver Jublee Tower a.k.a SJT is one of the academic blocks of VIT. It was inaugurated when VIT completed 25 years hence it is named silver jubilee.It was inaugurated by honorable Shri G.K Vasan, Minister for Shipping, Govt. of India on 29th September, 2009 in the presence of honorable Shri Pranab Mukherjee, former Finance Minister and the Ex-President of our country. Apart from enormous and spacious classrooms, brilliantly designed smart classrooms and galleries add to the list of unique features of SJT.
