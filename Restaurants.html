Dominoes: This outlet is located inside the VIT campus near SJT. They have ample space to sit and enjoy the food. They also deliver food to various blocks in VIT.

VIT Main Canteen: It is a nice place to have food especially for breakfast. Both veg and Non-veg meals are available. They maintain a good and hygienic environment. At peak hours it will be overcrowded. Air conditioned dining halls are also available.Overall a good place to eat.

FC Restaurant: Cheap and good quality food is available here. Really great for students who are looking for a quick bite between classes. Different varieties of dishes are present and service is good as well.

Arasan SJT Canteen: Canteen is located on the ground floor of the SJT building of VIT. Ambience and cleanliness is good and it is maintained very well.

Amul Ice Creams: A good place for icecream within campus. Although they don't have all the varieties but they still have a decent collection of icecream flavours to satisfy cravings on a hot summer day.
